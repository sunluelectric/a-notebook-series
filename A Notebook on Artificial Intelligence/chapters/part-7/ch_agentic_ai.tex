\chapter{Agentic AI} 

Agentic AI describes a comprehensive AI system that can make decisions and perform actions for either general or specific tasks, with minimum human intervention. It is an important building block of autonomous systems.

The concept of agentic AI is not new. However, in the past few years it has drawn increasing attentions due to the advent in LLM. LLM provides a general-purpose language interface for an agentic AI, and makes it more scalable and cost-efficient. Therefore, agentic AI is included in this part of the notebook.
